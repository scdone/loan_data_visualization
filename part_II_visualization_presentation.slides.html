Visual Analysis of Prosper Loan Data¶

by Stetson Done¶

Investigation Overview¶

Through this investigation, we will see how borrower credit scores affect both the borrower and the lender. We'll answer the following questions through data analysis and visualization:

1) How does a borrower's credit score affect their annual interest rate?
2) How does the borrower's credit score affect the loan risk for the lender?
3) How do the borrower credit score distributions vary between current loans and defaulted loans?

Dataset Overview¶

This dataset is comprised of loan and borrower data from Prosper, a peer to peer lending company.

How does a borrower's credit score affect their annual interest rate?¶

Using the pearsonr calculation and a seaborn regression plot, I found there is a moderate negative correlation between the borrower's loan interest rate and their credit score. As the borrower's credit score increased, their annual interest rate decreases.

The correlation coefficient was -0.4 with a p-value of 0, which indicates the results are statistically significant.

How does a borrower's credit score affect the estimated loan risk for the lender?¶

Using the pearsonr calculation and a seaborn regression plot, I found there is a moderate negative correlation between the borrower's loan interest rate and the estimated loan risk for the lender in case of loan charge off. As the borrower's credit score increased, the estimated risk of the loan decreases.

The correlation coefficient was -0.5 with a p-value of 0, which indicates the results are statistically significant.

How do the borrower credit score distributions vary between current loans and defaulted loans?¶

The borrower credit score distribution was higher on current loans and lower on defaulted loans. In fact, the mean borrower credit score on current loans ranged from 698 - 717, while the mean borrower credit score on defaulted loans ranged from 620 - 639.

The median borrower credit scores for current loans ranged from 700 - 719 and the median borrower scores for defaulted loans ranged from 640 to 659.
